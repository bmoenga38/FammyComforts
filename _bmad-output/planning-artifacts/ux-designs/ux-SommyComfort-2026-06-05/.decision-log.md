### Domain decisions (from Brian, 2026-06-05)

1. **Online payment model = "Guest chooses."** At booking the guest may (a) pay-now a **deposit OR full** amount via M-Pesa, or (b) **reserve and pay at the desk** (no online payment; booking is created pending-unpaid).
2. **Cancellation/no-show = refundable minus a fee.** A paid deposit is refundable less a cancellation fee; the fee is configurable. No-show treatment follows the same fee model (to confirm exact %).
3. **Reservation unit = specific room.** Guests/desk book an exact room (e.g. Room 103); availability is per-room. → drives the double-booking conflict state and the Postgres exclusion-constraint recommendation.
4. **M-Pesa pending/fail = block-until-confirmed on the pay-now path**, BUT with a mandatory **timeout escape hatch**: on STK failure/timeout the guest is never stranded — they get Retry, or fall back to "reserve & pay at desk." The block only applies when the guest chose pay-now; reserve-at-desk never blocks.
